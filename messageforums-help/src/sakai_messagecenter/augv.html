Viewing and managing Private Messages

On the Message Center screen, the Private Message Area is always on top, and the Discussion Forums area is on the bottom.

Note: If you don't see the Private Messages area, your site leader may not have activated private messaging. For instructions on activating private messaging, site leaders should consult Activating Private Messages.

The Private Message Area is intended for private correspondence with other individuals or groups in your site. Messages are organized into default Received, Sent, and Deleted folders.

The following basic functions are available in the Private Message Area:

  • To open an isolated view of the Private Message Area, click Private Messages.

    Note: If you can't see anything under "Private Message Area", try clicking the triangle next to "Private Message Area" to expand the area.

  • To compose a message, click Compose Private Message. For more information, see Sending and replying to Private Messages.

  • To create a new personal folder, click New Folder.

  • To forward your private messages to an email address of your choosing, click Settings. Next to "Auto Forward Private Messages", click Yes, then type the desired email address in the text box provided, and then click Save Settings.

  • The total number of messages and unread messages for each folder is displayed next to the folder's title. To see the contents of any folder, click the folder's name.

Inside a folder you will see a list of messages arranged by date.

  • To sort messages, click the relevant column title for Subject, Date, or Label.

  • To change the way messages are displayed, next to "View", use the drop-down list, and select All Messages or By Conversation.

  • To view another folder, click Previous Folder or Next Folder.

  • To compose a new message, click Compose Message. For more information, see Sending and replying to Private Messages.

  • Click the subject of a message to view its contents.

Inside a message you will see the message contents and other details.

  • To view another message, click Previous Message or Next Message.

  • To compose a reply, click Reply to message. For more information, see Sending and replying to Private Messages.

  • To move a message to another folder, click Move, use the radio buttons to select a destination folder, and then click Move Message.

  • To delete a message, click Delete. To confirm the deletion, click Delete.
