Messages and Forums notifications

To see if someone has added a new message or forum posting to one of your sites, go to your Home page in My Workspace. Under "Messages and Forums Notifications", you'll find a list of your sites, and the number of new messages and forum postings for each. If there are no new messages or forum postings, you'll see "None" beside your site.

Note: Messages and Forums notifications for a particular site are also displayed on that site's Home page.

Under "Message and Forums Notifications" in My Workspace, the following functions are possible:

  • Clicking Messages takes you to a Messages Received folder.

  • Clicking the icon next to the number of messages marks those messages as read.

  • Clicking the site name takes you to that site.

Under "Message and Forums Notifications" on your site's Home page, the following functions are possible:

  • Clicking Messages takes you to a Messages Received folder for that site.

  • Clicking Forums or the number of forums messages takes you to the forums for that site.
