Messages and Forums notifications

Note: "Messages & Forums Notifications" will only appear on your site's Home page if these tools have been added to your site.

To see if someone has added a new message or forum posting to one of your sites, go to your site's Home page. Under "Messages and Forums Notifications", you'll see Messages and/or Forums listed, along with the number of new messages and forum postings for each.

  • Clicking Messages takes you to the Messages Received folder for that site.

  • Clicking Forums or the number of forums messages takes you to the forums for that site.
